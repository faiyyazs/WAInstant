Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how WA Instant (“we”, “our”, or “the App”) handles data. If you have questions, contact us at support@example.com.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or sell personal information. WA Instant processes all data locally on your device. The App does not run remote servers to store user data.

Temporary data: when you select text (for example, a phone number) and invoke the App, that selected text is received temporarily by the App only to perform the requested action (e.g., open WhatsApp). The App does not persist or transmit that text to any third party.

2. How We Use Information

  • To detect phone numbers in the text you provide.
  • To open WhatsApp or other messaging apps as requested by you.
  • To show local confirmation dialogs or validation UI.

All processing is done locally on your device. We do not backup, sync, or analyze selected text on our servers.

3. Third-Party Services & Advertising

WA Instant displays advertisements using Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers, approximate location, usage data, and diagnostic information to deliver personalized or non-personalized ads.

We do not share the text you process (including phone numbers) with Google or any advertiser. All selected text is processed locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

For details on how Google processes data for advertising, please see the Google AdMob Privacy Policy:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

When you open WhatsApp from the App, you leave WA Instant and the WhatsApp privacy policy applies.

4. Security

Because we do not store user data centrally, there is no server-side personal data to secure. We recommend that you keep your device secure, use a lock screen, and avoid installing untrusted apps.

5. Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top reflects the current version. Significant changes will be communicated via the App or on our website.

7. Contact

If you have questions or requests regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@example.com.
